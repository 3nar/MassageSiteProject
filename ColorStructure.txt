


## Color Scheme
### Primary Colors:
1. **Muted Olive Green**: `#697565`
   - Used for buttons, highlights, and accents.

2. **Soft Beige**: `#ECDFCC`
   - Background color for sections, cards, and larger areas.

3. **Dark Olive Gray**: `#3C3D37`
   - Used for text, headers, and footers.

### Secondary Colors:
1. **Light Olive Tint**: `#A8B093`
   - Subtle backgrounds or hover effects.

2. **Cream White**: `#F5F2EB`
   - Secondary backgrounds for contrast.

### Gradient:
- **From**: Muted Olive Green (`#697565`)
- **To**: Soft Beige (`#ECDFCC`)
  - Applied to buttons, hero sections, and some headers for a calming effect.
